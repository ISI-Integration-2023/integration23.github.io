Your body is an instrument that you play to the symphony of dance. This Solo-Dance event has provided year-after-year a stage for the amalgam of Indian and Western Dance categories, where top 10 participants will out-perform each other in Indian/Western Category to lose themselves to this symphony of Dance.

Rules and Regulations
  1. There will be two separate categories for Eastern and Western dance forms. Fusion dance is not allowed.

  2. The performances can either be Solo or Duet.

  3. Particpants must be atleast 14 years of age as on 31st January, 2023.

  4. Participants must reach Registration Desk atleast 30 minutes before the scheduled time of event.

  5. Each participant must bring valid school or college ID Card in order to be considered for prizes.

  8. Vulgarity of any kind would lead to instant disqualification from the event. Hence, if the participant feels that the outfit/performance can be deemed as vulgar, it is strongly advised to consult the organisers before putting it on stage. The descion of the organisers will be final in case of any disputes arising due to this reason.

  9. The top 10 performances after the prelims in each category will advance to the final round. The finalists will be asked to perform from a set of songs suggested by the judges and will be alloted on a lottery basis.

  10. The organisers reserve the right to change any of the rules, timings and other details at any point of time. In case of any dispute, the decsion of the organisers will be final.

Model United Nations

Where the power of speech shall wipe out the inconsistencies in reasoning.

Model United Nations (MUN)



Integration 2023 presents the ISI MUN 23, the first of its kind at the Indian Statistical Institute. We, the MUN committee are excited to present the Itinerary for ISI MUN 23.

The aim of this MUN conducted by the students of ISI is to spread knowledge about the issues in the world, look at from the perspective of different countries and encourage the spirit of healthy discussions and debate.

Event Poster



Prize Pool

  1. Best Delegate from Each Committee: Rs. 500


Date & Timings
  1. Date: 4th & 5th February, 2023
  2. Time: 10 AM onwards


Registration

Registration Fees: Rs. 100 per Participant







Rules and Regulations

  1. Each participant shall be a delegate of a Member State in a committee.

  2. The contest shall constitute of 5 committees viz., The Security Council (UNSC), The Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), NATO, The Human Rights Committee (UNHRC) and The International Press (IP). The agendas of all committees shall be announced later.

  3. The rules of procedure shall be the same as the UNA-USA rules of procedure.

  4. Each committee will have a chairperson who will conduct the committee sessions and judge the performance of the delegates. All the rules of procedures specific to a committee will be explained by the chairperson before the committee session starts.

  5. Representatives of the International Press will be alloted different committees to which they will have an access to. The proceedings of the committees will be closely observed and reported by the International Press representatives.

  6. The organisers reserve the right to change any of the rules, timings and other details at any point of time. In case of any dispute, the decsion of the organisers will be final.

Alaap (Singing)



Event Poster



Prize Pool: Rs. 8000

  1. Solo: Rs. 3000
  2. Duet: Rs. 5000


Date & Timings

  1. Date: 4th & 5th February, 2023
  2. Time: 10 AM onwards


Registration





  1. Solo: Rs. 150
  2. Duet: Rs. 250 (Rs. 125 for each participant)

  3. If you and your partner want to perform both solo performances individually, and a duet performance togther, we have a special discount for you! Just pay Rs. 225 individually, instead of Rs. 275 (150 for Solo & 125 for Duet). Thus, you and your partner have to pay a total of Rs. 450 only instead of Rs. 550.

  4. Note that the above discount is available only if you are filling the form for Solo & Duet.


Rules and Regulations

General Rules:


  1. Participants must belong to the age group 15 to 35 years.

  2. Each participant must carry some valid ID proof.

  3. Particpants have to give either solo or duet performances.

  4. Duration : maximum 10 minutes (for both solo and duet).

  5. Recorded tracks are allowed. The participant has to submit the recorded track in a CD. It is advisable to bring a copy in a pen-drive to avoid unforeseen difficulties.

  6. The judges' choice will be taken as final.

  7. The organizers have the right to disqualify any participant if they find any on-stage comment or gesture illicit or inappropriate to the occasion.


Instruments:


  1. Participants can be accompanied by instruments and are required to bring their own instruments. No other instrument except harmonium, tabla or synthesizer will be provided by the organisers. Electronic Tabla / Electronic Tanpura are also considered as instruments. We will be providing Tabla, Keyboard, Guitar players for accompanying the participants. One must contact beforehand if he/she needs any of these.

  2. Solo: Each participant can either use a recorded track OR maximum two instruments at a time. If the participant plays any instrument, it will be counted as one. Maximum two persons will be allowed including instrumentalist.

  3. Duet: Each group can either use a recorded track OR maximum two instruments at a time. If the participant plays any instrument, it will be counted as one. Extra instrumentalists apart from the singers will not be allowed, i.e., exactly two persons will have to perform.

  8. The senders of the selected photographs will be notified through email and phone. The registration fee for the selected photographs will be Rs. 125/- each and must be done by 1st February, 2023.

  9. Only duly registered photographs will be considered for the final judgment.

  10. The final judging and announcement of results will be done by 3rd February 2023 by a panel of Judges.


    Short Video:

  1. Short videos are to be mailed at photography.integration.isi@gmail.com. You may submit the videos by sharing a drive link with access. Note that once submitted, the video cannot be changed.

  2. The video should have a length of at max 30 seconds, which may be a Slo-Mo, a Montage, a Reel or moments captured of nature or any events.

    4. The senders of the selected videos will be notifies through email and phone.

  5. Only duly registered photographs will be considered for the final judgment.
Participants have to accept and confirm that the photographs/videos belong to him/her.
