Montage

Filmmaking is a chance to live many lifetimes. It is a miracle of collaboration.
Montage

General Rules:

  • Each participant must submit ONE disc (CD/ DVD/ Blu-ray) which won't be returned to the participant.
  • In the main directory of the submitted disc, there should be a folder called "Montage 2018". Any other file or folder in the main directory will be ignored.
  • In the "Montage 2018" folder of the main directory of the submitted disc, there should be two files naming - "entry.avi" and "info.txt"- the absence of either resulting in direct disqualification; a file called "entry.srt- if found - will be recognized; everything else in the folder will be ignored.
  • The file "info.txt" should clearly mention the participants name. It can include any optional information about "entry.avi".
  • Only if the duration of "entry.avi" does not exceed fifteen minutes, it will be judged as a short fiction film with "entry.srt" ( if exists) as the subtitle file.
  • Judge's decision will be final in case of any dispute arises.
  • Anyone from outside Kolkata mail the "Montage 2018" folder with all the required contents ("entry.avi", "info.txt") mentioned above to example@gmail.com.

Rangasaj

"Painting is concerned with all the 10 attributes of sight; which are: Darkness, Light, Solidity and Colour, Form and Position, Distance and Propinquity, Motion and Rest." ~ Leonardo da Vinci
Cartooning

General Rules:

  1. Participants should be aged between 15years and 30 years.
  2. All participants must carry a valid photo-ID proof.
  3. Time allotted for each participant is a maximum of 4 hours, starting at 1pm. Light refreshments would be provided to the participants (without interruptions in the programme)
  4. Each participant will work on only one art work without the boundaries of any theme. We encourage the idea of being true to yourself.
  5. Participants need to specify which paper they wish to use at the time of registration itself. Full size cartridge paper or handmade paper would be provided to them, and the participants can resize it according to their own requirements.
  6. Each participant shall bring along the necessary drawing/painting materials as required.
  7. Medium of painting will be water colour/poster colour/acrylic/pastel/charcoal/pen & ink/mixed media.
  8. The art work would be displayed during the entire course of Integration and shall be returned to them on the evening of 3rd Feb 2019(Sunday). If you wish to take back your art work, please collect it from the event heads on the aforementioned date.
  9. The decision of the jury shall be final.

Registration Fee:INR 50/-

Prize money worth 5000/-

The organizers reserve the right to change any of the rules, timings and other details such as prize money at any point of time. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organizers is final.
