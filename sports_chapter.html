Gully Cricket

Come have a shot at your favourite game at your favourite sports fest Things are bound to get competitive especially when it's a matter of pride.

Gully Cricket


Event Poster



Prize Pool: Rs. 8000

Registration Fees

Rules and Regulations
  1. Each innings will be 10 overs. Maximum of 2 bowlers can bowl maximum 3 overs and every other player can bowl maximum 2 overs.

  2. There are 2 powerplays, a Mandatory Powerplay and a Batting Powerplay.

    1. In Mandatory powerplay there will be maximum 8 players of fielding team in the ground. This powerplay will be the first over of the innings.

    2. In the batting powerplay maximum 9 players of fielding team will be present. It can be taken in any of the overs from 2-10 according to the will of batting team. If it is not taken within 9th over. 10th over will automatically be the batting powerplay.

  3. Each team should have 10 players and max 3 subs. Substitute may field for at most 2 overs.

  4. Sixes are not allowed, if a ball goes out of boundary without touching the ground, any person other than the batsman or wickets then the batsman will be given out.

  5. Batsman should not be holding the 'plate' of the bat.

  6. The bowler should be bowling with his elbow bend, from the side of his/her head, without revolving his arm and not taking any more than 2 steps.

  7. There is a speed limit of bowling. If it crosses that limit and the batsman appeals for it within 3 seconds and umpire agrees then it will be called a ’fast’ ball and the ball would be considered dead. If a bowler bowls any more than 2 ’fast’ bowls in a single over then those extra ’fast’ bowls will be given no ball, as in the ball will not be counted(runs scored in that ball will also not be counted) but an extra of 1 will be awarded to the batting team.

  8. In case of a tie there will be a super-over. In case there is a tie, it would be broken by super-half-over, bowl-out(5 balls), toss in that order.

  9. Each team should have there own equipments(e.g. bats). Organisers might provide equipments but one team should not count on it.

  10. Decision of the field umpire stays. Any argument with the umpire will not be tolerated.
  11. All other standard ICC rules will be applicable.
  12. Points system:
    1. 2 points if a team wins.
    2. 0 point if a team loses.
    3. 1 point will go to each of the team in case of a match is cancelled.
  13. Group rank will be decided on the following basis (in that order):
    1. Points
    2. Net Run Rate
    3. Head-to-head (result of normal match or tie-breakers)
    4. Coin toss
  14. Unsporting behaviour of any player may result in the direct disqualification of the team.
  15. Decision of the organizers will be final in any matter.

The football tournament gives you a chance to show off your skills and stamina on the field and also compete with some of the best football teams in the city.

  2. Knockout Stage: 21st and 22nd January, 2023 10 a.m. onwards


Registration
    Registration Fees:Rs. 3000 for each team


Rules and Regulations
  1. Each team should have a proper jersey set. Each player should have jogger/studs. Keds/running shoes will not be allowed on field.

  2. Decision of the field referee stays. Any argument with the referee will not be tolerated.

  3. All matches will consist of 15 minutes half and 5 minutes rest in between the halves.

  4. All other FIFA rules will be applicable.

  5. If a player gets two yellow cards in consecutive two matches (or a straight red card in a single match), he will be banned for the next match.

  6. Points system:
    1. 3 points if a team wins in normal time or in penalties (to be held after normal time).
    2. 0 point if a team loses in normal time or in penalties (to be held after normal time).
    3. 1 point will go to each of the team in case of a draw.
  7. Group rank will be decided on the following basis (in that order):
    1. Points
    2. Goal difference (during normal play)
    3. Goals scored (during normal play)
    4. Head-to-head (result of normal match or tie-breakers)
    5. Coin toss
  8. Goals scored during the tie-breakers shall not be counted in goal difference.
  9. Unsporting behaviour of any player may result in the direct disqualification of the team.
  10. Decision of the organizers will be final in any matter.
